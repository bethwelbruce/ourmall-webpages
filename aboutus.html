About Our Mall

Discover the story behind our mall.

Our History

Founded in 1990, Our Mall has grown from a modest shopping center into one of the region's premier shopping destinations, offering an unparalleled selection of stores and services.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an exceptional shopping experience by offering a diverse mix of high-quality stores and entertainment in a family-friendly environment.

Unique Features

  • Over 300 stores including international and local brands.
  • State-of-the-art cinema complex.
  • Indoor and outdoor dining options featuring global cuisines.
  • Regular events, from fashion shows to cultural festivals.
  • Family entertainment centers and playgrounds.

Community Involvement

We're committed to giving back to our community through various initiatives, including charity events, local artist showcases, and educational programs.
