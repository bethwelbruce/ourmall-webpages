Your Questions Answered

What are the mall opening hours?

Our Mall is open from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM from Monday to Saturday, and 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM on Sundays.

Where can I find parking information?

Parking is available in the mall's multi-level parking facility. The first two hours are free, and charges apply thereafter. For more details, visit our Parking Information page.

Are there any dining options in the mall?

Yes, Our Mall boasts a variety of dining options ranging from fast food to gourmet restaurants. Visit our Dining section for more information.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, Our Mall offers gift cards that can be used in any store within the mall. They are available for purchase at the customer service desk.
